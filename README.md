# Hi, I'm Gulzar Ali

CFD Engineer & SciML Researcher at BetaCodes.

I work at the intersection of computational fluid dynamics and scientific
machine learning — building tools that make high-fidelity physics simulations
faster, smarter, and deployable in real engineering environments.

---

## What I Work On

- **Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs)** — neural networks that respect
  the laws of fluid mechanics, not just the data
- **Neural Operators** (FNO, DeepONet) — learning solution operators that
  generalize across geometry and boundary conditions
- **Reduced Order Models (ROM)** — compressing full-order simulations into
  low-dimensional representations without losing physical fidelity
- **Turbulence & Thermal Modeling** — data-driven closure models and CNN-based
  surrogate frameworks for complex flow prediction
